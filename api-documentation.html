RowCast API Documentation

Real-time rowing conditions and forecast data

Base URL: https://api.samwduncan.com

Weather Endpoints

GET
/api/weather
Retrieve complete current weather data including temperature, wind conditions, humidity, and atmospheric pressure.
GET
/api/weather/current
Get current weather summary with essential conditions for quick reference.
GET
/api/weather/forecast
Weather forecast data for upcoming conditions planning.
GET
/api/weather/extended
Extended weather forecast with detailed hourly and daily predictions.

Water Conditions

GET
/api/water
Complete water conditions including level, flow rate, and temperature data.
GET
/api/water/current
Current water level and flow conditions for immediate reference.
GET
/api/water/predictions
Predicted water conditions and level changes.

RowCast Scoring

GET
/api/rowcast
Current RowCast score based on all weather and water conditions.
GET
/api/rowcast/forecast
RowCast scores for upcoming time periods with detailed conditions.
GET
/api/rowcast/forecast/simple
Simplified forecast with timestamps and scores only.
GET
/api/rowcast/forecast/short-term
Next 24 hours of RowCast scores with detailed analysis.
GET
/api/rowcast/forecast/extended
Extended forecast showing conditions for the next several days.

GET

RowCast Scoring Algorithm

Primary Factors (Most Important):

Wind Speed
Lower wind speeds score higher (primary factor)
Wind Direction
Headwinds and tailwinds preferred over crosswinds
Water Level
Optimal range for safe navigation and speed

Secondary Factors:

Temperature
Comfortable range for athletic performance
Humidity
Moderate levels preferred for comfort
Visibility
Clear conditions for safety
UV Index
Moderate UV levels (safety consideration)
Safety Alerts
Weather warnings, visibility, lightning potential

Score Interpretation:

8.0 - 10.0
Excellent rowing conditions
6.0 - 7.9
Good conditions with minor considerations
4.0 - 5.9
Fair conditions - exercise caution
0.0 - 3.9
Poor conditions - not recommended
